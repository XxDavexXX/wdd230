Advanced Technology Chamber of Commerce (ATC) - Site Plan

Purpose

The Advanced Technology Chamber of Commerce (ATC) aims to be a comprehensive center that promotes excellence, innovation and growth in the advanced technology sector. Its mission encompasses the facilitation of collaborations, the provision of resources and support, as well as the defense of the interests of companies and professionals operating in this field. The ATC is dedicated to creating an enabling environment for business development, facilitating meaningful connections, offering professional development opportunities, and promoting the adoption of best practices across the advanced technology industry.
